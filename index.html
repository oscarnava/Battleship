Battleships

Instructions:

  • Left grid is the user's board and right grid is the computer's board
  • You can edit your ships distribution by dragging them around.
  • Toggle the ship's orientation by double clicking on it.
  • Start the game by initiating attack by clicking on the computer's board.
Select play level: Easy Normal Hard

When enabled, the board will show the calculated probabilities for a "good shot".

Darker color means bigger probability.
